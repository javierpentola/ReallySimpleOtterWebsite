Interesting Facts About Otters

Otters are fascinating creatures that live both on land and in water. They are known for their playful behavior and are a favorite among wildlife enthusiasts.

Habitat

  • Location: Found in various regions around the world including rivers, lakes, and coastal areas.
  • Environment: Prefer clean, unpolluted water bodies with abundant food supply.

Physical Characteristics

  • Streamlined bodies for efficient swimming
  • Thick fur to keep them warm in cold water
  • Webbed feet for better movement in water
  • Sharp claws and teeth for catching prey

Diet

  1. Fish: The primary diet of otters, providing essential nutrients.
  2. Crustaceans: Includes crabs and crayfish, which are rich in protein.
  3. Insects: Often consumed by younger otters as they learn to hunt.
  4. Small Mammals: Occasionally hunted by larger otters.
